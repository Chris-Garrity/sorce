The Lighthouse

Alison Moore

The novel deals with a middle-aged man's memories of childhood abandonment as he embarks on a walking trip through Germany.

Jenn Ashworth of The Guardian called the novel "a chilly, heart-wrenching story".

Swimming Home

Deborah Levy

The short novel deals with the experiences of poet Joe Jacobs, when his family vacation is interrupted by a fanatic reader.

Julia Pascal, reviewing the novel for The Independent, describes the author's storytelling as "allusive, elliptical and disturbing."
