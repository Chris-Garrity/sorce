The Tower Of London

The history of the Tower

The Tower of London has played a prominent role in English history. It was besieged several times and controlling it has been important to controlling the country.

The Tower was oriented with its strongest and most impressive defences overlooking Saxon London. Archaeologist Alan Vince suggests it was deliberate.
